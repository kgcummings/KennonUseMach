WELCOME.

We have come here today to mourn what we have lost-- This is a space to honor our fallen images. Be silent as you walk throughout the corridors, but keep your mind roaring with thoughts of good will and respect for those who now lie below you.




2006 - 2009

Found on the now-defunct website of a Brooklyn-based Italian bakery. This was an image of the bakery's owner-- an older portly gentleman with balding grey hair holding a dish of tiramisu. He's smiling wide, using his free hand to point at something out of frame. This image left us when the bakery this was made for closed down following a death in the owner's family. We mourn the bakery. We mourn this image.
